Frequently Asked Questions

What is Git Achievements?

Git Achievements is a service-based hook for Github and Bitbucket that allows developers, designers and program managers alike to record all the achievements they acquire, their colleagues, or just random strangers whom they're secretly stalking. Compete against friend and foe alike, or just have fun using the service.

Achievements can be added by anyone at anytime, providing a limitless bank of possible achievements to unlock and badges to be won. If you prefer privacy, you can set up a local service for your business or startup and just point the endpoint to that.
